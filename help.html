Codeverse2024 is a 24-hour hackathon where participants from diverse backgrounds come together to create innovative solutions to real-world problems. It's a platform for coders, designers, and innovators to collaborate, learn, and showcase their skills.

Anyone interested in coding, innovation, and creativity can participate. This includes students, professionals, and enthusiasts from various fields such as computer science, engineering, design, and more. We encourage individuals of all skill levels and backgrounds to join us.

Yes, the registration fee is 500 INR. This fee helps us cover event expenses, provide food and drinks, and offer prizes to winners.

The event will take place on 5th and 6th of October at Vidyavardhini's Bhausaheb Vartak Polytechnic, Vasai Road West. The venue is easily accessible by public transportation, and we'll provide detailed directions on our website.

Yes, you can bring your own equipment, such as laptops, tablets, or software. However, we'll also provide necessary equipment, including internet access, and collaboration tools (sponsored).

A team must have minimum 2 members and maximum of 4 members. We encourage teams to have diverse skill sets and backgrounds to foster creativity and innovation.

Our problem statements will be based on AI-themed challenges, focusing on innovative solutions that leverage artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science. However, to accommodate participants with varying skill levels, we will also include some easier-to-address problem statements that still align with our AI theme.

All problem statements, regardless of complexity, will be evaluated based on the same criteria: innovation, impact, design, and technical complexity. This ensures a level playing field, where participants are judged on their creative approach, solution effectiveness, user experience, and technical expertise. Our judges will assess each solution's overall value, rather than its complexity, to reward outstanding ideas and execution.

Yes, problem statements will be available on our website before the event, i.e. at 12 AM on 5th October. Participants can review and prepare in advance to maximize their hackathon experience.

Yes, we will provide a variety of food and drinks throughout the event, including meals, snacks, and refreshments. Additionally, a paid snacks corner will be available at night time.

Vegetarian cuisine will be available.

Yes, the dedicated dining area will be located in the college canteen.

No, outside food and drinks are not allowed.

Yes, a coffee and tea station will be available.
